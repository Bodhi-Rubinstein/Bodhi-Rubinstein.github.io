Welcome to My Website

Hi! My name is Bodhi and I am a junior at the University of Colorado, Boulder, studying Computer Science. I'm very passionate about high performance computing (HPC); I work at the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) as a part of the HPC Research and Development team, developing cloud resources, a testbed cluster, and various other projects. I'm very interested in pursuing a career in HPC administration, networking, and security. I have previous experience working with HPC resources as part of a student development team for the NSF Advanced Cyberinfrastructure Coordination Ecosystem: Services and Support (ACCESS) project as well as a member of the RMACC student cohort. I love to knit, ski, and play video games in my free time!
